Al-Aqsa Mosque is the second mosque placed on the earth after the Grand Mosque. Muslim narrated on the authority of Abu Dharr, may God be pleased with him, who said: I asked the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace, about the first mosque that was placed on the earth, and he said: “The Sacred Mosque.” I then said what? He said: "Al-Masjid Al-Aqsa," I said, how many between them? He said: "Forty years, then the land is yours a mosque, so wherever prayer catches you, pray."
